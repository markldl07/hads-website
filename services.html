Our Services

Our Mission

We work to improve the lives of abandoned dogs by volunteering at shelters, educating our community, and inspiring compassion through hands on service.

What We Do

HADS members visit animal shelters weekly, help with cleaning and feeding, socialize dogs, and support adoption efforts through photos, events, and outreach.

Adoption Support

We help showcase adoptable dogs by taking photos, writing profiles, and connecting shelters with potential adopters.

Community Events

HADS hosts school wide and local events to raise awareness about Korea’s abandoned animal crisis and encourage responsible pet ownership.
